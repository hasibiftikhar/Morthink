## Annotations

- **This page is now separate from About Us**, as it should be. Mixing a company story page with a transactional contact form confuses the page's purpose and hurts both SEO and user experience, since search engines and visitors expect a contact page to do one job.
- **Contact details are placed high on the page**, before the form, since B2B buyers often want to call or message directly rather than fill out a form, especially across the time zones and regions in your target markets (Europe, Middle East, Central Asia).
- **Section 4 exists to reduce form clutter.** Your site already has a dedicated Agent & Distributor Application Form with its own specific fields (business type, years in business, monthly volume, and so on). A visitor who already knows they want to become a partner should not fill out a generic contact form and wait for someone to redirect them; they should go straight to the right form.
- **No live chat, office address, or hours are included**, since none of those were provided in your source material. These are noted as an open item below.
