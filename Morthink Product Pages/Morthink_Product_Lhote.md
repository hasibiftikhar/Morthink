### Section 2: Protection Function

**Header:** What Lhote Protects Against

*[Image: Stone chip or scratch impact on unprotected paint vs. protected paint]*

Lhote protects the paint underneath it from the everyday damage that shortens a vehicle's finish life. The film takes the impact so the paint does not have to.

**Scratches and Stone Chips**

*[Image: Close-up of stone chip impact absorbed by the film surface]*

Lhote absorbs minor scratches and stone chip impacts. The surface takes the hit. The paint underneath stays untouched.

**Light Scratch Self Healing**

*[Image: Scratch disappearing on film surface with heat or sunlight]*

Light scratches and swirl marks on the film surface disappear on their own when the coating warms up. Sun exposure or a warm water rinse is enough. No polishing needed.

**Hydrolysis and Yellowing**

*[Image: Side-by-side of non-yellowing white vehicle with film vs. yellowed film without]*

Lhote is resistant to hydrolysis and yellowing. White cars stay white. The film holds its optical clarity over time, not just in the first months after install.

**Contamination and Water Spots**

*[Image: Water beading or surface contamination rolling off the film]*

The hydrophobic and hydrophilic coating on the surface resists fouling and water spot buildup. Contaminants have a harder time bonding to the film, so the surface stays cleaner between washes.

### Section 3: Performance

**Header:** How Lhote Performs During and After Install

**Easy to Stretch and Install**

*[Image: Installer stretching Lhote film over a large hood panel]*

Lhote is engineered for large hood applications. It stretches enough to follow panel contours cleanly, without over stretching past the safe limit. Installers working on large hoods get full coverage in one piece with no seam line.

**Large Format Coverage**

*[Image: 6ft roll or wide panel showing full hood coverage without seam]*

The 1.83m width covers most full hoods in a single pass. No splicing two narrower sheets together. No visible seam line across the center of the hood. The install is cleaner and the result looks better.

**Paint Safe Adhesive**

*[Image: Clean removal or no-residue adhesive demonstration]*

The adhesive grips the paint surface firmly during install and holds securely in use. If the film ever needs to come off, it releases cleanly. No marks, no residue on the paint.

**All Weather Adaptability**

*[Image: Vehicle in varied climate conditions -- rain, heat, cold]*

Lhote is designed to perform in varied climates. It does not become brittle in cold conditions or lose adhesion in heat. The film performs the same way regardless of where it is installed.

**Optical Clarity and Color Fidelity**

*[Image: Film on black and white vehicle showing clarity and color enhancement]*

Lhote maintains superior optical clarity after install. It enhances deep blacks and keeps whites from yellowing. The color fidelity you deliver on install day holds over the life of the film.

### Section 5: Applications

**Header:** Where Lhote Is Used

*[Image: Large SUV, truck, or performance car with full hood coverage]*

Lhote is sized for vehicles with large hoods and long panel runs where standard width film would require splicing. It is used on:

**Full Hood Coverage**

*[Image: Large hood covered in one piece]*

Vehicles with wide hoods that standard 5ft film cannot cover without a seam. Lhote's 6ft width eliminates that join.

**Long Panel Applications**

*[Image: Long side panel or roof panel installation]*

Long roof panels, extended side panels, and any surface where splicing would create a visible seam or where over stretching a narrower roll would risk film failure.

**High End and Show Vehicles**

*[Image: Luxury or show vehicle with clean finish]*

Vehicles where seam lines are unacceptable. When the final result needs to look factory, Lhote's wide format eliminates the visible evidence of the install.
