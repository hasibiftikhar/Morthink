### Why Choose Malu LE75

**Header:** Long-lasting Protection, Easy Installation, without yellow on white vehicles, High Stretchability

7.5 mil, quick self healing film with hydrophobic coating. Like every MoShiEx paint protection film, Malu LE75 is built from five tested layers: a protective top layer, a self healing coating, a TPU substrate, an adhesive layer, and a release liner.

- Superior optical clarity with excellent color fidelity and gloss, enhancing black while keeping white clear and non yellowing
- Outstanding self healing performance with strong scratch resistance and high stretchability for easy application
