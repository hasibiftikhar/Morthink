### Why Choose Malu FE75

**Header:** Long-lasting Protection, Easy Installation, Ultimate Color Fidelity, High Gloss, Excellent Stain Resistance, Easy to Maintain, Excellent Stretchability

7.5 mil coating engineered for the highest color fidelity in the Malu line. Like every MoShiEx paint protection film, Malu FE75 is built from five tested layers: a protective top layer, a self healing coating, a TPU substrate, an adhesive layer, and a release liner.
