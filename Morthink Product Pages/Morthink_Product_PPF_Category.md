### Section 2: Protection Functions

**Header:** What MoShiEx PPF Protects Against

Every MoShiEx paint protection film is built to shield your vehicle's paint from the physical and environmental damage that builds up over time. Each protection function works on its own. Together they keep the paint looking the way it looked when the vehicle left the showroom.

**Scratch and Stone Chip Protection**

*[Image: Stone chip impact absorbed by PPF surface, paint underneath undamaged]*

The film takes the impact so the paint does not. MoShiEx PPF absorbs minor scratches and stone chips on contact. The vehicle's original paint stays untouched beneath the film.

**Self Healing**

*[Image: Light scratch disappearing from PPF surface under heat or sunlight]*

Light scratches and swirl marks on the film surface heal on their own. Heat from sun exposure or a warm water rinse is enough to restore the surface. No polishing, no labor, no additional cost.

**Yellowing Resistance**

*[Image: White vehicle with PPF staying white vs. yellowed unprotected surface over time]*

MoShiEx PPF resists yellowing over time. White cars stay white. The film holds its optical clarity from the day it goes on. Long after cheaper films have turned, MoShiEx keeps the paint looking right.

**Hydrolysis Resistance**

*[Image: Water or moisture contact with film surface showing no degradation]*

The film is engineered to resist hydrolysis, the breakdown that happens when water and heat work on unprotected film over months and years. This resistance is built into the formulation, not added as a surface coating that wears off.

**Stain and Contamination Protection**

*[Image: Water beading on hydrophobic PPF surface, contaminants rolling off]*

The hydrophobic surface coating resists fouling, water spots, and environmental contamination. Contaminants have a harder time bonding to the surface, so the film stays cleaner between washes.

**UV Protection**

*[Image: UV rejection diagram or faded unprotected panel vs. protected panel]*

MoShiEx PPF maintains high UV rejection across all series. This prevents the paint beneath the film from fading and keeps the film itself from degrading under prolonged sun exposure.

### Section 3: Performance Features

**Header:** How MoShiEx PPF Performs

The way a film performs during install and in daily use is as important as what it protects against. MoShiEx PPF is engineered for both the installer and the end user.

**Easy to Stretch and Install**

*[Image: Installer stretching PPF over a panel curve, smooth conforming result]*

MoShiEx PPF stretches past 280 percent without tearing. It follows real curves and contours without fighting the installer. Wrapping edges cleanly is built into the material, not dependent on technique alone.

**Paint Safe Adhesive**

*[Image: Clean removal of PPF showing no residue left on paint surface]*

The adhesive grips the paint surface firmly during install and holds securely in daily use. When the film comes off, it releases cleanly. No marks, no residue, no damage to the original paint.

**Superior Optical Clarity**

*[Image: Close-up of PPF on vehicle showing clear, non-distorting surface]*

MoShiEx PPF maintains high optical clarity after installation. VLT of 89 percent or higher across standard series. The film does not distort the color or appearance of the paint beneath it.

**Color Enhancement**

*[Image: Deep black vehicle with PPF enhancing the gloss and depth of color]*

MoShiEx PPF enhances the finish it protects. Black stays deeper. White stays brighter. Gloss values above 95 GU across standard series mean the installed result looks better than the bare paint.

**All Weather Adaptability**

*[Image: Vehicle in varied climate conditions, cold, tropical heat, heavy rain]*

MoShiEx PPF performs consistently across climate zones. It does not become brittle in cold environments or lose adhesion in sustained heat and humidity. The film works the same way in tropical climates as it does in cold dry ones.

**Long Term Durability**

*[Image: Multi-year installation showing film still clear and intact]*

Resistance to hydrolysis, yellowing, UV, and staining means the protection holds over years, not just months. The film you install today performs the same way when the client comes back next year.
