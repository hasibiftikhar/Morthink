### Why Choose Magal

**Header:** Nano Ceramic Technology, Built for Clarity

Magal blocks infrared heat using nano ceramic layers, reducing cabin temperature without the light scattering that causes haze in lower grade ceramic film. Uniform particle distribution keeps visibility crystal clear, and visible light reflectivity is built to closely match factory glass, minimizing windshield glare and internal reflections. Inorganic rare earth materials give Magal strong corrosion resistance, so the film resists fading over years of daily use.
