### Why Choose Wolfra

**Header:** Precious Metal Layers, Built for Performance

Wolfra reflects solar infrared heat using precious metal layers, keeping the cabin cooler without relying on nano ceramic particles that can scatter light and cause haze. Visible light reflectance is built to closely match factory glass, so windshield glare and internal reflections stay to a minimum. An optimized alloy coating gives Wolfra long term resistance to corrosion and oxidation, so performance does not fade over years of sun exposure.
