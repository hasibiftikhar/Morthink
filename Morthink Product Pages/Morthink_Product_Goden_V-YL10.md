### Why Choose Goden V/YL10

**Header:** Strong Protection, Easy Installation, All-weather Performance

10 mil, heat activated self healing film with hydrophilic and hydrophobic anti fouling and water spot resistant coating, crafted for luxury vehicles. Like every MoShiEx paint protection film, Goden V/YL10 is built from five tested layers: a protective top layer, a self healing coating, a TPU substrate, an adhesive layer, and a release liner.
