### Section 2: What Architecture Film Does

**Header:** The Same Technology, a New Surface

*[Image: Commercial building glass or interior glass surface]*

The coating and film technology Morthink developed for automotive paint protection and window film applies directly to the challenges buildings face. Heat management, UV protection, and surface protection are the same problems on a larger scale.

Our architecture film line is designed to bring measurable performance to building glass, interior surfaces, and exterior applications. The product platform, manufacturing standard, and testing approach are the same ones behind every MoShiEx automotive product.

**Heat and Solar Management**

*[Image: Building exterior with solar film managing heat load]*

Architecture film reduces solar heat gain through glass. This lowers the cooling load on the building, reduces energy consumption, and makes interior environments more comfortable without blocking the view.

**UV Protection for Interiors**

*[Image: Interior space with sunlight visible but UV managed]*

UV radiation fades furniture, flooring, and interior finishes over time. Architecture film blocks the UV component of sunlight without significantly reducing visible light transmission. The interior stays bright. The contents stay protected.

**Surface Protection**

*[Image: Glass or surface with protective film applied]*

Protective film applied to glass and surfaces in high-traffic areas reduces scratch damage, graffiti, and the cost of replacing damaged panels. The film takes the damage. The surface underneath stays intact.
