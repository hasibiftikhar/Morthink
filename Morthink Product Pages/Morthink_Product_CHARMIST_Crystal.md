### Why Choose CHARMIST Crystal

**Header:** We Define TPU Color Film With Technology

CHARMIST Crystal is built with a cast grade color layer, so the color stays stable through stretching and will not fade or peel away from the base film. Our DEPLOTEC color platform controls batch to batch color consistency, so a roll ordered this month matches a roll ordered next year. Over 400 colors are available across the CHARMIST line, with custom color development on request.

Like every MoShiEx paint protection film, Crystal is built from five tested layers: a protective top layer, a self healing coating, a TPU substrate, an adhesive layer, and a release liner, so color change never comes at the cost of protection.
