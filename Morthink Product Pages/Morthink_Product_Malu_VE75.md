### Why Choose Malu VE75

**Header:** Long-lasting Protection, Easy Installation, no yellow on white vehicles, For Hot & Rainy Tropical Climates

7.5 mil, heat activated self healing film with hydrophilic, high anti fouling and water spot resistant coating, built for tropical climates. Like every MoShiEx paint protection film, Malu VE75 is built from five tested layers: a protective top layer, a self healing coating, a TPU substrate, an adhesive layer, and a release liner.
