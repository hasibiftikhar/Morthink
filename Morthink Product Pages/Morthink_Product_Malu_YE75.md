### Why Choose Malu YE75

**Header:** Long-lasting Protection, Easy Installation, without yellow on white vehicles, All-weather Performance

7.5 mil, heat activated self healing film with hydrophobic, high anti fouling and water spot resistant coating. Like every MoShiEx paint protection film, Malu YE75 is built from five tested layers: a protective top layer, a self healing coating, a TPU substrate, an adhesive layer, and a release liner.

### Performance
