### Section 3: We Show You What's Inside the Film

**Header:** Five Layers, Explained, Not Hidden

Every MoShiEx paint protection film and TPU wrap film is built from five layers: a protective top layer, a self healing coating, a TPU substrate, an adhesive layer, and a release liner. Most brands stop at "self healing technology" and leave it there. We explain what each layer does, what we test it against, and why it is built the way it is. That level of detail is not common in this industry, and we think it should be.
