### Why Choose Malu BE75

**Header:** Long-lasting Protection, Easy Installation, Without yellow on white vehicles, Excellent Stretchability

7.5 mil, ultra stretch coating built for high temperature application conditions. Like every MoShiEx paint protection film, Malu BE75 is built from five tested layers: a protective top layer, a self healing coating, a TPU substrate, an adhesive layer, and a release liner.
