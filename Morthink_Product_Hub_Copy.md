### Section 4: Paint Protection Film

**Header:** Six Tiers, Built for Every Budget and Every Vehicle

Our paint protection film comes in six product lines, each built for a different job. Lhote is our wide format film, made for large hoods without splicing or over stretching. Goden is our luxury tier, crafted for high end vehicles. Chomo delivers race track level protection for performance cars. Malu is our classic line, with the widest range of finishes, from gloss to matte to deep black, plus color specific variants tuned for hot, cold, or humid climates. Kanga is our deluxe tier, built for long lasting protection in tough conditions, including hot and rainy tropical climates. Choyu is our practical tier, offering cost effective protection without compromising the core features that make MoShiEx film reliable in the field.

Every paint protection film line shares the same core promise: it stretches enough to install cleanly, it heals light scratches on its own, and its adhesive releases cleanly if it ever needs to come off.

**CTA:** Explore Paint Protection Film

### Section 5: CHARMIST TPU Color Film

**Header:** We Define TPU Color Film With Technology

CHARMIST is our colored paint protection film, built on a PPF grade TPU base. It gives vehicle owners a way to protect their paint and change their color at the same time, without giving up the protection a clear film provides.

CHARMIST is built with a cast grade color layer, so the color stays stable through stretching and will not fade or peel away from the base film. Our DEPLOTEC color platform controls batch to batch color consistency, so a roll ordered this month matches a roll ordered next year. Over 400 colors are available, with custom color development on request.

**CTA:** Explore CHARMIST Color Film

### Section 6: Window Film

**Header:** Heat Rejection Without the Haze

Our window film uses two different technologies to block heat while keeping the glass clear. One line uses magnetron sputtered metal layers for the highest heat rejection and the lowest reflectivity. The other uses nano ceramic layers, free of the particle scattering that causes haze in lower grade ceramic film. Both are built to match factory glass reflectivity, so you do not get glare or ghosting on the windshield.

**CTA:** Explore Window Film
