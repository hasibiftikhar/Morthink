### Section 3: The AEGOR Technology Platform

**Header:** Four Capabilities. One Platform.

The AEGOR platform brings four distinct capabilities together. All four work in parallel across every product we make for OEM partners.

**Testing Data Bank**

*[Image: Data, documentation, or test records visual]*

We have accumulated testing data across climate environments, adhesive formulas, and coating performance over decades. When a new product enters development, we draw from this bank rather than starting from zero. Your product benefits from every product that came before it.

**Scientific Evaluation**

*[Image: Evaluation process or materials analysis illustration]*

Every material combination goes through a structured evaluation before it enters production. We assess mechanical performance, optical clarity, adhesion strength, and climate durability against real targets, not estimates. If a combination does not pass evaluation, it does not become a product.

**Climate Adaptation**

*[Image: Climate chamber or diverse environment testing]*

Our formulas are tested against real climate conditions, from tropical heat and humidity to cold dry environments. An OEM product that performs well in one market may fail in another if the formula is not tuned for local conditions. We build climate testing into the development process, not as an afterthought.

**Local Adaptation**

*[Image: Regional market or custom product development scene]*

We adjust formulas, widths, and specifications to match regional installation practices and market preferences. An OEM product built for the European aftermarket may need different adhesion characteristics than one built for Southeast Asia. We handle that adaptation before the product reaches your market.

### Section 5: IATF 16949 Certification

**Header:** Certified Quality Management, Not Just a Badge

*[Image: IATF 16949 certificate alongside quality inspection in progress]*

Morthink holds IATF 16949 certification, the quality standard automakers trust most. We manage production under this standard from start to finish, which means our documentation, our processes, and our corrective actions are all held to automotive grade requirements.

For OEM partners building products for the automotive aftermarket, this certification matters. Your customers expect their suppliers to meet the same standard their own automotive clients hold them to. We do.

We also hold ISO 9001 (quality management), ISO 14001 (environmental responsibility), and ISO 45001 (occupational health and safety) certifications.

*[Image: Quality documentation, inspection records, or issue-tracking process visual]*

If a quality question comes up after delivery, we can trace it back to the exact production stage it happened. This is not a general promise. The paper trail exists for every batch we ship.
