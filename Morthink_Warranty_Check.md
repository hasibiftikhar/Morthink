### Section 2: How the Warranty Works

**Header:** How MoShiEx Warranties Work

*[Image: Electronic warranty platform or digital warranty registration screen]*

MoShiEx film is manufactured under IATF 16949 certification. Every batch passes three quality checks before it ships. Every batch ships with a Certificate of Analysis and a full inspection report.

When a certified MoShiEx installer completes an installation, they register the job through our electronic warranty platform. The registration links the installation to the specific batch of film used. If a quality issue comes up after installation, the record is there. The batch can be traced back to the exact production stage.

**What the warranty covers**

A valid MoShiEx warranty covers quality defects that trace back to the film itself: delamination, adhesive failure, yellowing beyond expected degradation, or other manufacturing-related issues under normal use conditions. It does not cover damage caused by improper installation, physical impact, incorrect maintenance products, or improper removal.

**Who can register a warranty**

Warranty registration is completed by the MoShiEx certified installer at the time of installation, not by the end customer. If your installer has not registered your installation, contact them directly or use the contact form below to reach our team.
