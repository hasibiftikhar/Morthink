### Section 2: Company Introduction

**Header:** A Film Manufacturer Built on Real Engineering

Morthink is the company behind MoShiEx. We are based in Guangdong, China, with production sites in Zhaoqing City and Qingyuan City. Our team is 120 people strong, led by senior experts in coatings and material science.

We design and manufacture paint protection film, TPU wrap film, window film, and architecture film. Every product comes from one process and technology platform we built ourselves, called AEGOR. AEGOR combines 70+ years of coating technology with our own manufacturing know how across two factories. Together, we produce 500,000 rolls of film a year, covering TPU casting, color casting, and coating in house.

### Section 6: Built for Automotive Grade Consistency

**Header:** Dust-Free Workshops. Full Process Control. One Standard.

Our film is made inside dust-free workshops built specifically for coating, curing, and roll cutting. We control the process from raw material arrival through final inspection. Every order moves through three quality checks: incoming material testing, in process inspection during manufacturing, and final testing before it ships. Every batch gets a Certificate of Analysis and an inspection report, so what you receive matches what we tested.

**Production Bases:** We operate three major production bases designed to keep output consistent and delivery fast, no matter the order volume or the season.

**Quality Consistency:** Modern equipment, high-standard dust-free workshops, and process discipline mean the roll you order today performs the same way as the roll you order next year.

### Section 7: IATF 16949 Certified

**Header:** Every Meter of Film Meets Automotive Standards

Morthink holds IATF 16949 certification, the quality standard automakers trust most. We manage production under this standard from start to finish, so every meter of film that leaves our factory meets true automotive grade requirements.

We also hold ISO 9001 (quality management), ISO 14001 (environmental responsibility), and ISO 45001 (occupational health and safety) certifications, all three issued the same day and maintained through active surveillance audits.
