### Section 4: The AEGOR Technology Platform

**Header:** Five Layers of Engineering in Every Roll

Most film looks simple from the outside. Ours is built from five layers, and every layer is tested and tuned on its own before it ever becomes one product.

- **Protective top layer:** Controls clarity, smoothness, and how easily the layer above it releases during install
- **Self healing coating:** Resists stains and yellowing, keeps its gloss over time, and heals light scratches on its own
- **TPU substrate:** The core layer, built for clarity, UV resistance, and the right amount of stretch
- **Adhesive layer:** Controls how well the film grips the surface and how cleanly it releases if removed
- **Release liner:** Protects the adhesive layer until the moment of install

We test each layer against real numbers, not guesses. Our coatings are built to reach 45 to 60 degrees Celsius before self healing kicks in, and to heal in five seconds or less. Our film is built to stretch past 280 percent elongation without failing, so installers can wrap real curves without the film tearing.

## Annotations

- **No duplicate content with the homepage.** The homepage states the same facts (factories, output, certification) in one line for scanning. This page unpacks each one into its own section, since About Us visitors are further into research mode and want depth, not a repeat of the same paragraph.
- **The five layer breakdown comes straight from your company deck**, not from general industry knowledge. This is a genuine differentiator most competitor sites will not have, since it shows real engineering depth instead of marketing language.
- **Mission, vision, and values are used close to your original wording** but reworded for grade 6 readability and shortened sentence structure, since your deck's original phrasing runs long for web copy.
- **The tech origin line still says "United States and Japan,"** matching the homepage. This is still an open question, see below.
- **Real numbers replace vague adjectives everywhere they are available:** 45 to 60 degree self healing threshold, 5 second heal speed, 280 percent elongation, 120 employees, 500,000 rolls a year.
- **This page no longer includes contact details or a contact form.** That content now lives on its own Contact Us page, since combining the two was a mistake in the previous version. About Us should end on a CTA that sends the visitor toward becoming a dealer or exploring products, not a form.
