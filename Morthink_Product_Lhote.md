### Why Choose Lhote

**Header:** One Piece Coverage for Large Panels

Most paint protection film forces installers to splice smaller sheets together on large hoods, or stretch a narrower roll further than it should go. Lhote solves both problems. It comes in a 6 foot by 50 foot roll, wide enough to cover most hoods in a single piece, with no seam line and no risk of over stretching the film past its safe limit.

Lhote uses a heat activated self healing coating with hydrophobic and hydrophilic properties, plus high anti fouling and water spot resistance built in. Light scratches and swirl marks disappear on their own when the coating warms up, whether from sun exposure or a warm water rinse.

### Product Structure

**Header:** What's In the Film

Lhote is built from the same five layer construction behind every MoShiEx paint protection film: a protective top layer, a self healing coating, a TPU substrate, an adhesive layer, and a release liner. Each layer is engineered and tested on its own before it becomes part of the finished roll.
