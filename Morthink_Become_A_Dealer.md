### Section 3: Product Features

**Header:** What Makes Each Product Category Different

**Paint Protection Film**

MoShiEx PPF stretches past 280 percent without tearing, so it wraps real curves without over-stretching or fighting the installer. The adhesive grips firmly during installation and releases cleanly when the film needs to come off. White cars stay white. Dark cars stay dark. The film resists hydrolysis and yellowing, so the protection holds long after the point of sale.

*[Image: PPF installation or finished result on vehicle]*

**Window Film**

Our window film blocks heat without blocking the view. One line uses magnetron sputtered metal layers for high heat rejection and low reflectivity. The other uses nano ceramic layers free of the particle scatter that causes haze in lower grade ceramic film. Both are built to match factory glass reflectivity. No glare, no ghosting on the windshield.

*[Image: Window film installation or heat rejection demonstration]*

**CHARMIST Color Film**

CHARMIST is built on a PPF grade TPU base, so your customer gets a color change and paint protection in one product. The color layer is cast grade. It stays bonded to the base film under stretching and will not fade or peel away. The adhesive is paint-safe. Over 400 colors are available, with custom color development on request.

*[Image: CHARMIST color film on vehicle, showing color and finish quality]*

**Architecture Film**

Our architecture film brings the same coating expertise behind our automotive products to glass and surfaces in buildings. Full product details for this line are coming soon. Contact us for current availability.

- **Section 2 leads with three verifiable brand principles**, not adjectives. Each principle (materials matched to process, real-world design balance, manufacturer pricing) is grounded in actual claims from the product spec sheet and company deck.
