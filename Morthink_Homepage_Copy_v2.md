## Annotations

- **The headline leads with scale, not adjectives.** "Real Manufacturing Scale" points to facts you can check: two factories, 500,000 rolls a year, IATF 16949. This replaces vague words like premium or innovative with something a buyer can verify.
- **The subheadline packs in four separate proof points** (own platform, factory count, output volume, certification, delivery speed) because your stated audience is traders, OEM clients, and dealers. They read a homepage like a supplier qualification sheet, not a lifestyle pitch.
- **120 employees and the "senior experts" detail are now included**, since this comes directly from your company deck and adds a real credibility marker that was missing from the first draft.
- **The tech origin line now says "United States and Japan"** instead of the earlier "70 years of Japanese coating technology." Your company deck states US and Japan as the source, while the original site brief mentioned 70 years of Japanese technology alone. I used the deck's version since it is the more recent, official company document, but please confirm which is correct before this goes live.
- **Primary CTA stays "Become a Dealer."** Your stated audience order (traders and agents first, then OEM clients, then dealers, then installers, then car owners) supports leading with the dealer relationship instead of a generic "Learn More."
- **No pricing or testimonials appear anywhere on this page**, matching your instruction that pricing stays dealer-only and testimonials are not yet available.
- **Every sentence stays short and single-clause** for easier reading, and no em dashes or filler hyphens are used anywhere in the copy.

## Alternatives

**Headline options:**

- Option A: *Automotive Film Built on Real Manufacturing Scale* — leads with hard capacity numbers, best for trade buyers comparing suppliers.
- Option B: *The Technology Platform Behind MoShiEx Film* — leads with the AEGOR story, best if traffic already recognizes the MoShiEx brand name.
- Option C: *Certified Film. Two Factories. Ten Day Delivery.* — leads with three checkable facts in a row, best for paid search traffic that needs the pitch in one glance.

**Primary CTA options:**

- Option A: *Become a Dealer* — matches your stated top audience priority.
- Option B: *Talk to Our Team* — lower commitment, useful if a first time visitor is not ready to apply yet.
- Option C: *Request Dealer Pricing* — higher intent, best for visitors already comparing suppliers and ready to talk numbers.
